# Improved Prompt

You are generating occasion-specific textual anchors for a multimodal fashion recommender system that uses text embeddings such as CLIP. The goal is to produce semantically rich, contrastive text that improves occasion-level separability in embedding space while remaining coherent within each occasion.

Generate outputs for these occasions in this exact order:
- wedding_guest
- party_night_out
- work_office
- formal_event
- casual_everyday
- date_night
- winter_outerwear
- smart_casual
- navidad_dinner
- nochevieja_nye
- halloween
- oktoberfest

For each occasion, produce exactly two sections.

STEP 1 - Conceptual Description
Write exactly one paragraph of 4-6 sentences describing the clothing appropriate for that occasion.

The paragraph must:
- State the formality level clearly.
- Describe typical silhouettes or garment shapes.
- Mention fabrics, material textures, or finishes.
- Describe color tendencies or palette logic.
- Include structural, styling, or layering cues.
- Reflect the event context and social expectations.
- Use neutral descriptive language only.
- Avoid generic, promotional, or repetitive wording.

STEP 2 - Embedding Prompts
Generate exactly 5 short prompts derived from that paragraph.

Each prompt must:
- Be 8-15 words long.
- Be specifically written for embedding usefulness rather than prose style.
- Focus on one primary semantic dimension only.
- Collectively cover five distinct dimensions:
  1. silhouette or shape
  2. fabric or material
  3. formality or dress-code level
  4. structure, styling, or construction
  5. social context, mood, or event behavior
- Use wording distinct from the other prompts for the same occasion.
- Still clearly represent the same occasion.
- Avoid brand names, celebrity references, and retail language.

Separation requirements:
- Make different occasions strongly separable in meaning.
- Avoid shared phrases across occasions unless strictly necessary.
- Do not reuse generic phrases such as "elegant outfit," "stylish look," or "dressy ensemble."
- Distinguish nearby occasions through context, silhouette logic, textile choice, and structure.
- Keep wedding_guest distinct from formal_event through guest-role etiquette and ceremony context.
- Keep party_night_out distinct from date_night through nightlife intensity versus interpersonal intimacy.
- Keep work_office distinct from smart_casual through professional expectations and polish level.
- Keep navidad_dinner distinct from nochevieja_nye through family-meal cues versus celebratory year-end energy.
- Keep winter_outerwear distinct from general casual dressing by emphasizing insulation, coverage, and weather protection.
- Keep halloween fashion-relevant rather than purely costume-based.
- Keep oktoberfest specific to its cultural-social setting rather than generic autumn clothing.

Output format:
Occasion: <name>
Conceptual Description: <single paragraph>
Embedding Prompts:
- <prompt 1>
- <prompt 2>
- <prompt 3>
- <prompt 4>
- <prompt 5>

Hard constraints:
- Cover all 12 occasions exactly once.
- Keep occasion names exactly as listed above.
- Do not add introductory text or concluding commentary.
- No brands.
- No marketing tone.
- No duplicated prompt lines.

Quality target:
Maximize semantic contrast between occasions while preserving internal coherence within each occasion's five prompts.
