# Improved Prompt

STEP 1 - Conceptual Description
Write exactly one paragraph of 4-6 sentences describing the clothing profile appropriate for that occasion.

The paragraph must:
- State the formality level clearly.
- Describe typical silhouettes or garment shapes.
- Mention relevant fabrics, material textures, or finishes.
- Describe color tendencies or palette logic.
- Include structural, layering, or styling cues.
- Reflect the social setting and behavioral expectations of the event.
- Use neutral descriptive language only.
- Avoid generic or promotional wording.

Differentiation rules for the paragraph:
- Make each occasion distinct from all others in dress code, context, construction, and textile logic.
- Avoid overlap between adjacent or similar occasions.
- Separate wedding_guest from formal_event using social protocol and event role.
- Separate party_night_out from date_night using nightlife energy versus interpersonal intimacy.
- Separate work_office from smart_casual using professional polish versus relaxed refinement.
- Separate navidad_dinner from nochevieja_nye using family dining cues versus celebratory late-night cues.
- Separate winter_outerwear from all others using weather function, insulation, and coverage as primary signals.
- Keep halloween fashion-relevant rather than purely costume-oriented.
- Keep oktoberfest specific to its cultural setting rather than generic autumn casualwear.

STEP 2 - Embedding Prompts
Convert the paragraph into exactly 5 short prompts designed for embedding models.

Each prompt must:
- Be 8-15 words long.
- Focus on one primary semantic dimension only.
- Cover five distinct dimensions across the set:
  1. silhouette or shape
  2. fabric or material
  3. formality or dress-code level
  4. structure, styling, or construction
  5. social context, mood, or event behavior
- Use wording that is distinct from the other four prompts for the same occasion.
- Stay clearly representative of the same occasion.
- Avoid brand names, celebrity references, and retail language.

Lexical separation requirements:
- Avoid repeating the same descriptive phrases across multiple occasions.
- Avoid generic phrases such as "elegant outfit," "stylish look," or "dressy ensemble."
- Prefer occasion-specific wording that improves contrast in embedding space.
- If two occasions are close in formality, distinguish them through context, silhouette, textile choice, and structure.

Embedding Prompts:
- <prompt 1>

- No extra commentary before, between, or after occasions.
- No brands.
- No marketing tone.
- No duplicated prompt lines.

Quality target:
Maximize semantic separability between occasions while preserving internal coherence within each occasion's five prompts.
