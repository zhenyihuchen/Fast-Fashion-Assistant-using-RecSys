Occasion: wedding_guest
Conceptual Description: Wedding guest attire is semi-formal to formal and polished without competing with the couple. Typical silhouettes include midi dresses, tailored jumpsuits, or refined suits with clean lines. Fabrics lean toward crepe, chiffon, satin blends, or lightweight wool, avoiding overly casual textures. Color tendencies favor muted pastels, jewel tones, or soft neutrals while steering clear of bridal whites. Structural cues include modest coverage, balanced proportions, and understated detailing rather than dramatic cutouts or heavy embellishment.

- Semi-formal guestwear with midi silhouettes and refined tailoring
- Crepe, chiffon, or satin-blend fabrics suited to ceremonies
- Muted pastels and jewel tones for celebratory guest attire
- Polished lines with modest coverage and restrained detailing
- Formal social context: ceremony-appropriate, non-bridal clothing
